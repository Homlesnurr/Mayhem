## Opening game

The game is opened by running the 'main.py' file. No libraries other than pygame and numpy is used.

## Guide

Opening the game brings up the main menu, pressing the "Play" button starts the game. Cliking the Escape key will bring back the main menu.

The goal of the game is to shoot the enemy ship to gain points, while avoiding being hit, or running into solid objects. Being hit results in a score deduction, and hitting an enemy ship with a bullet will result in gaining points.

Thrusting will cause your fuel gauge to decrease, when it reaches $0$, you will no longer be able to thrust the ship forward and you will fall. To avoid this, there are $2$ respawning fuel barrels that refill your fuel gauge.

## Controls

For player 1, press $\textrm{W}$ to thrust the ship forward, press $\textrm{A}$ and $\textrm{D}$ to turn the ship left and right, and press $\textrm{S}$ to fire a bullet.

For player 2, press $\textrm{↑}$ to thrust the ship forward, press $\textrm{←}$ and $\textrm{→}$ to turn the ship left and right, and press $\textrm{↓}$ to fire a bullet. (Arrow keys)
